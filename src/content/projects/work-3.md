### Description

<br>

Designed specifically for mobile-first platforms, this vertical motion ad promotes Lifeline Shop's seasonal giveaway. The project utilizes Lifeline's signature blue branding mixed with festive elements—like animated string lights and kinetic typography—to create a sense of urgency and excitement for the $600 gift card prize.
